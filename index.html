Japan Trip 2025

Two best friends, Julien and Theo decided in mid 2024 to travel to Japan. The plan was simple: three weeks to discover the rising sun and immerse themselves in the rich culture and history of this fascinating country.

They decided to book from 22 of April to 15 of May 2025. For a budget of around 3,500€ per person.

More than a trip, it truly enhanced life's vision throughout the discovery of a humble and magninificient culture.
